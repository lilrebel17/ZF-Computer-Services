The Mission

We aspire to help our community first and foremost. I want to be able to use my knowledge of electronics and IT to give people a face they can trust without having to worry about breaking the bank or getting ripped off by some shop wanting seventy dollars just to diagnose a device. I have been around this industry for over five years and have never liked the way most shops charge an arm and a leg just to get in the door. I know how important it can be to keep your pc up and running, and hate the predatory tatics of some shops. Thats why I created ZFix to help combat those people and hopefully make the industry I love a better place, and keep my community up and running.

How do we do that?

The formula is simple. We treat everyone like were fixing computers it for our friends. We understand the need to keep your devices up and running and want you to leave feeling the same way you would if your friend down the street did it for you
